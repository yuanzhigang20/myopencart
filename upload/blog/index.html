FAQ

Quick answers for privacy, materials, beginner choices, and care.

Will packaging show adult product names?

Discreet packaging should avoid explicit product names or adult branding on the outside of the parcel. Check the store policy before ordering if privacy is important.

Is discreet shipping available?

Privacy-focused stores usually use plain outer packaging and neutral delivery information. If you are unsure, contact support before checkout and ask how the parcel and label will appear.

What materials are body-safe?

Common body-safe options include medical-grade silicone, ABS plastic, glass, and stainless steel when clearly labeled and easy to clean. Avoid vague material claims, strong odors, or porous materials without care guidance.

How should beginners choose their first product?

Start with simple controls, clear materials, manageable size, easy cleaning, and privacy features that match your comfort level. A calm first purchase is usually better than the most complicated option.

How should intimate products be cleaned and stored?

Follow the product instructions, use compatible mild soap or cleaner, dry fully, and store items separately in a clean pouch away from lint, heat, and incompatible materials.
