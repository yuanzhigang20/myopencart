Usually no. Beginners benefit from simple controls, lower settings, easy cleaning, and clear materials more than maximum power.

Is toy cleaner required every time?

Not always. Many non-porous products can be cleaned with mild soap and water if the instructions allow it. Cleaner is useful when compatible and convenient.

A privacy-focused shop should use plain outer packaging and avoid product names on the outside label. Check the shipping page or contact support before ordering if this matters.
