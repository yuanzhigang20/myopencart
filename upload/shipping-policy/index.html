Shipping Policy

Discreet delivery

Orders are prepared for adults 18+ and shipped with plain outer packaging whenever possible. Product names are not printed on the outside of the parcel.

Processing and tracking

Most orders are processed after payment confirmation. When tracking is available, details are sent to the customer email used at checkout.

Privacy support

If you need help before ordering, contact our support team through the contact page so we can answer shipping, packaging, and delivery questions discreetly.
